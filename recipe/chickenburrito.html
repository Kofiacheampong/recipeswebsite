Grilled Chicken Burrito

Description

A delicious grilled chicken burrito with a perfect blend of flavors and textures.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 cup cooked rice
  • 1 cup black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 cup corn kernels
  • 1 cup diced tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup diced onions
  • 1/4 cup chopped cilantro
  • 1 cup shredded cheese (cheddar or Mexican blend)
  • Tortillas for wrapping
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional toppings: sour cream, guacamole, salsa

Procedure

  1. Season the chicken breasts with salt and pepper.
  2. Grill the chicken until fully cooked. Let it rest for a few minutes before slicing into strips.
  3. Assemble the burritos by placing a tortilla on a flat surface.
  4. Layer rice, black beans, corn, tomatoes, onions, cilantro, grilled chicken strips, and cheese on the tortilla.
  5. Optional: Add toppings such as sour cream, guacamole, and salsa.
  6. Wrap the burrito tightly, securing the ends.
  7. Repeat for additional burritos.
